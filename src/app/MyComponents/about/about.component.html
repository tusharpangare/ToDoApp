About Todo List

A simple yet powerful tool to help you organize your tasks and stay productive.

Features

  • Easy to Use: Simple and intuitive interface makes managing tasks effortless.
  • Customizable: Personalize your todo lists with colors, tags, and priority levels.
  • Reminders: Set reminders and due dates to stay on top of your tasks.
  • Collaborative: Share lists with friends, family, or colleagues for seamless teamwork.
  • Sync Across Devices: Access your todos from anywhere with cloud synchronization.
